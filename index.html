Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our honey

How do I know this honey is pure and authentic?

Our honey is sourced directly from beekeepers in Poonch valley. It's completely raw, unprocessed, and never heated. You can verify its purity through simple home tests like the water test or thumb test.

What makes Kashmir honey different from regular honey?

Kashmir honey comes from high-altitude regions (8000+ feet) where bees collect nectar from rare Himalayan wildflowers and medicinal herbs. This gives it a unique flavor profile and higher nutritional content compared to regular honey.

How do I place an order?

Simply click any "Order Now" button or the WhatsApp link to send us a message. We'll respond quickly to confirm your order and arrange pickup or personal delivery in Delhi.

How should I store the honey?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Honey never spoils - it may crystallize over time, which is natural. Simply warm it gently to return to liquid form.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders (10kg+) and corporate gifting. Contact us via WhatsApp for custom pricing and packaging options.
